## Key Sections

### Professional Summary

**Example:**
> Product Manager with 5+ years of experience launching data-driven products that drive growth. Led cross-functional teams to deliver features increasing MAU by 40% and revenue by $1.5M annually.

### Core Competencies

- **Strategy:** Roadmap planning, market research, competitive analysis
- **Execution:** Agile/Scrum, stakeholder management, sprint planning
- **Analysis:** Data-driven decision making, A/B testing, metrics tracking
- **Technical:** SQL, wireframing, API understanding, JIRA

### Professional Experience

**Strong Examples:**
- ✅ Launched 3 major features increasing MAU by 40% and revenue by $1.5M
- ✅ Led cross-functional team of 12 engineers and designers through agile sprints
- ✅ Defined product roadmap based on customer feedback and market research
- ✅ Increased user engagement by 35% through data-driven feature prioritization

## Tips for Product Manager Resumes

1. **Lead with impact:** Every bullet should show business outcomes
2. **Quantify everything:** Users, revenue, engagement, retention
3. **Show cross-functional leadership:** How you worked with eng, design, sales
4. **Include failed experiments:** Shows data-driven learning mindset

## Key Sections for a Data Analyst Resume

### 1. Professional Summary

Highlight your analytical expertise and business impact:

**Example:**
> Data Analyst with 4+ years of experience transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. Proficient in SQL, Python, and Tableau with proven track record of driving $2M+ in revenue through data-driven recommendations.

### 2. Technical Skills

**Languages:** SQL, Python, R
**Visualization:** Tableau, Power BI, Excel
**Databases:** PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB
**Tools:** Jupyter, Git, JIRA

### 3. Professional Experience

Use metrics to demonstrate impact:

**Strong Examples:**
- ✅ Analyzed customer behavior data identifying $2M in revenue opportunities
- ✅ Built interactive dashboards in Tableau reducing reporting time by 70%
- ✅ Automated ETL processes saving 15 hours/week of manual work

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Too technical:** Balance technical skills with business impact
2. **No metrics:** Always quantify your analyses and outcomes
3. **Missing soft skills:** Include communication, collaboration, and stakeholder management
